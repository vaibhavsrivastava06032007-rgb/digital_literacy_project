#  Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

> A simple guide to help students maintain a **safe, respectful, and positive online presence**.

---

##  Why This Matters
Social media can shape your identity. Following best practices helps you:
-  Protect your **privacy & security**
-  Build a **positive digital footprint**
-  Avoid scams, misuse, and conflicts

---

## ✅ Do’s

-  **Use strong & unique passwords**
-  **Update privacy settings regularly**
-  **Think before posting**
-  **Share positive & informative content**
-  **Respect others online**

---

## ❌ Don’ts

-  **Don’t share personal information**
-  **Avoid offensive or harmful content**
-  **Don’t click suspicious links**
-  **Don’t spread fake news**
-  **Avoid oversharing your location**

---

##  Checklist Table

| ✅ Do’s                         | ❌ Don’ts                          |
|--------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Strong unique passwords        | Share personal info              |
| Updated privacy settings       | Post offensive content           |
| Think before posting           | Click suspicious links           |
| Share positive content         | Spread fake news                 |
| Respect others                 | Overshare location               |

---

##  Pro Tips

-  Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
-  Review and clean up old posts regularly
-  Be mindful of your **digital footprint**
-  Report suspicious or harmful activity

---

##  Note
Following these practices ensures a **safe, respectful, and responsible** social media experience.

---

##  Conclusion

By following these guidelines, you can:
- Stay **secure online**
- Protect your **personal information**
- Build a **positive online identity**
